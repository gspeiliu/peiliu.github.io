I am LI Li, a PhD student in Software Engineering at the Interdisciplinary Security and Trust Centre (SnT), University of Luxembourg (UL), under the supervision of Prof. Yves Le Traon and Dr. Jacques Klein. I received my bachelor degree in Computer Science from Southwest University in 2010 and my master degree in Computer Science as well from Beihang University in 2013.

My research interests are in the areas of Android Security, Static Analysis, Machine Learning, and Empirical Study.
