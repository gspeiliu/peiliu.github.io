I am a PhD student in Software Engineering at the Interdisciplinary Security and Trust Centre (SnT), University of Luxembourg (UL), under the supervisor of Prof. Yves Le Traon and Dr. Jacques Klein. I earned a M.S. degree in Computer Science from Beihang University in 2013 and a B.S. degree in Computer Science as well from Southwest University in 2010.
My research interests are in the areas of Android Security, Static Analysis, Machine Learning, and Empirical Study.
